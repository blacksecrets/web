About the Band

Black Secrets is a tribute to the great Alice in Chains. Formed in 2024 in Winchester VA, they deliver an authentic and powerful recreation of the legendary Seattle sound.

In an recent interview Bobby stated "This has been a dream of mine, to find a group of talented musicians and put togther a show consisting of the early years of Alice in Chains music."

All four members have a great love and are deeply inspired by the orginal line up of Alice In Chains. They have incorporated the same elements of heavy metal music and tight harmonies. The members of Black Secrets have grasped the concepts enabling them to replicate the aspects of what made Alice In Chains such a great band. Their love and dedication to this music shows in every song performance right down to their technique, gear, and dress.

"We play this music because we love the raw energy it projects. We want the audience to feel the music the same way we do."

Reed Personius – Vocals
 Bobby Steelman – Guitar
Rich Burns – Bass
 Zach Johnson – Drums
