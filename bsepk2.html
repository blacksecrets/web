Black Secrets

A Tribute to Alice in Chains

About

Black Secrets is a dedicated tribute to the legendary grunge pioneers, Alice in Chains. Formed in 2024 in Winchester, VA, we bring a faithful and authentic recreation of the band’s iconic sound and intense live energy. Our tribute is born from deep admiration — Alice in Chains is simply the greatest band of their genre and era.

From the haunting harmonies to the heavy riffs, we stay true to the original sound while delivering a powerful stage experience that resonates with longtime fans and new audiences alike.
